About Project




Founded in 2009 by Korneev "YoGuRT" Sergey. The name of the project reflects the image of human morality decay in the epoch of industrial progress and global development. Including such subjects as Love and War, this project comes laden with incredible electronic symphonies, filled with the strongest cosmic scale atmosphere, that overwhelms the listener, giving no chance to escape the ocean of deep and illusion breaking emotions.


Mechanic Illusion album was created in classical industrial-rock style with the use of heavy guitar riffs and foot tapping electronic's combined with beautifully complex russian language.


The single Kalt Berlin 2014 is presented on this website in the run-up to the release of the new album! In the authors opinion, all the power and strength of the Great Germany and it`s Capital is enclosed in the single Kalt Berlin in original instrumental format (without vocal).
